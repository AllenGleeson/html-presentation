Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu or BJJ is a hobby I picked up 7 years ago. It helps me keep physically active and it is good for the mind.

  • Exercise

    It is great for keeping in shape by keeping things interesting as you are also doing a physical activity.

  • Technique

    It has a huge number of techinques that can always be refined so you are always improving.

  • Community

    Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu has a great community of people that you get to know very well as you train more and more.
